Parking Lot Crack Sealing

CRACK SEALING CAN LAST UP TO 8 YEARS

Crack Sealing is a popular choice to improve the longevity and durability of a parking lot. No single maintenance technique preserves and extends the life of pavement as effectively as asphalt crack sealing. If cracks are sealed as soon as possible or as soon as the appear, water infiltration will be minimized and extensive pavement repair avoided. Sealing cracks prevents water and moisture damage bellow your asphalt. It eliminates the most common cause of premature pavement deterioration.


What is crack sealing?

Crack sealing is the process of placing an adhesive sealant into cracks on the pavement surface, preventing the infiltration of moisture and non-compressible materials into the pavement.

Why choose this method?

Crack sealing is a more costly procedure for the pavement owner but provides substantial value due to its long life expectancy. While crack filling may only last one or two seasons depending on the cracks activity, crack sealing can last up to 8 years or more and is considered a permanent treatment.
